About Me

I am a graduate student at The University of Hong Kong, pursuing a Master's degree in China Development Studies. My academic interests lie at the intersection of communication, public policy, and data analytics.


Previously, I earned my Bachelor's degree in Broadcasting and Television from Dalian University of Technology (985/211), where I developed expertise in computational communication, media production, and social research methods.


I have interned at Hunan Broadcasting System and People's Daily Online, gaining hands-on experience in media strategy, content production, and integrated communication campaigns. My research focuses on digital media, aging discourse, and rural education equity.
